News Block

This draft page shows the authoring structure for the EDS news block. The block renders 4 cards per row, loads up to 4 rows total, and includes the fixed filters AI, Robotics, Technology, and Innovation.

The GNews API key is now expected in the worker environment, not in block authoring. Configure it in workers/website/.env for local development and as a Worker secret for deployment.

Authoring Notes
